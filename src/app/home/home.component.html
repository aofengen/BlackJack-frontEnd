WELCOME TO BLACKJACK!


House Rules:

  1. Blackjack pays 3/2.
  2. Dealer hits on 16, stays on all 17s.
  3. Maximum of three splits per hand.
  4. When splitting aces, you only get one additional card. If that card is an ace, you can split them again.
